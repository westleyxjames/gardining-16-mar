Pricing FAQs

Common questions about our pricing

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees! The prices shown include all standard services listed for each plan. Additional services or special requests may incur extra charges, which we'll always discuss with you upfront.

Can I switch plans?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Just let us know, and we'll adjust your service for the next visit.

Do you offer discounts for recurring services?

Yes! We offer special rates for clients who schedule regular weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly services. Contact us for more details on recurring service discounts.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, checks, and electronic bank transfers. Payment is due upon completion of service unless you have a recurring service agreement.
